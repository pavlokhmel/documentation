Training events

Norwegian Research Infrastructure Services NRIS (formerly known as the The Metacenter), has an extensive education and training program to assist existing and future users of our services. The courses are aimed to give the participants an understanding of our services as well as using the resources effectively. The courses are free of charges. Our upcoming courses are listed below:
